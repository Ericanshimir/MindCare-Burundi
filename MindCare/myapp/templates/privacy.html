Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. MindCare Burundi is committed to protecting your personal data and ensuring transparency in how we collect, use, and store information.

1. Data Privacy

We do not collect sensitive personal data unless you explicitly provide it. Any data submitted is anonymized and used only for service improvement and usage analytics.

2. Confidentiality

All anonymous chats are encrypted and stored temporarily for quality assurance. Only authorized mental health professionals can access relevant data — and only in cases where support is requested. Users are never identified unless they choose to share identifying details.

3. User Rights

  • You have the right to request the deletion of your chat history and any stored information.
  • You can opt out of non-essential communications at any time.
  • Requests can be made through your account settings or by contacting support.

4. Copyright and Ownership

All content is either original, licensed, or cited. It is protected under intellectual property laws. Users may not reproduce or reuse any materials without permission.

5. Data Sharing and Transparency

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. There are no hidden trackers or deceptive designs. Any cookies or analytics used are disclosed clearly.

6. Security

We use encryption and security best practices to ensure your data is protected. However, no system is entirely immune to breaches — use caution when sharing information online.

For questions about this policy or to request your data, please contact our support team directly.
